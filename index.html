Burger: A burger is a savory sandwich consisting of a grilled or fried patty made from ground meat, such as beef, chicken, or veggie alternatives, served in a sliced bun. It's often topped with lettuce, tomato, cheese, onions, pickles, and condiments like ketchup and mustard.

Noodles: Noodles are a staple food made from unleavened dough that is stretched, rolled flat, and cut into various shapes, such as strips or strings. They can be boiled, stir-fried, or served in soups, and are commonly made from wheat, rice, or buckwheat flour.

Rolls: Rolls are versatile and portable food items made by wrapping fillings, such as meats, vegetables, or cheese, in a thin, flatbread or dough. They come in various forms, including sushi rolls, spring rolls, and sandwich wraps, offering a convenient and customizable meal option.

Aaloo Muttar is a classic North Indian dish featuring tender potatoes (aaloo) and green peas (muttar) cooked in a flavorful blend of spices, onions, tomatoes, and aromatic herbs. This vegetarian delight offers a harmonious balance of textures and tastes, with the creamy potatoes complementing the sweet freshness of the peas. Aaloo Muttar is commonly enjoyed with rice or Indian breads like roti or naan for a satisfying and wholesome meal.

Biryani is a fragrant and flavorful rice dish originating from South Asia, typically made with long-grain rice, aromatic spices, and meat (such as chicken, mutton, or goat) or vegetables. The ingredients are layered and cooked together with saffron-infused rice, creating a rich and savory one-pot meal that's beloved for its complex flavors and textures. Biryani is often garnished with fried onions, fresh herbs, and a squeeze of lemon before serving, adding brightness and depth to this beloved dish.
